Inventory Dashboard

A modern inventory management dashboard built with Lovable, featuring interactive graphs and analytics for real-time inventory tracking. This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your stock levels, sales performance, and inventory metrics.

Features

- 📊 Interactive Dashboard- Visualize inventory data with dynamic charts and graphs
- 📈 Real-time Analytics - Monitor stock levels, sales trends, and inventory performance
- 🤖 AI Chatbot Integration- Powered by n8n to interact with your inventory data
- 🔄 Data Management - Read and update inventory information through the chatbot
- 📱 Responsive Design- Access your dashboard from any device

Chatbot Capabilities

The integrated AI chatbot can:
- Retrieve current inventory levels and stock information
- Update product quantities and details
- Provide insights and analytics on demand
- Answer inventory-related queries in natural language
